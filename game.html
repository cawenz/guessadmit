❓ HOW TO PLAY

Guess how selective each U.S. college is.

  • 🎯The goal. A college appears — guess what percent of applicants it admits (0–100%). The closer you land, the more coins you bank.
  • 🎚️Make your guess. Use the −10 / −1 / +1 / +10 buttons (or arrow keys: ←/→ = ±1, ↑/↓ = ±10), then LOCK IN.
  • 🪙Scoring. Each round is worth up to 1000 pts, scaled by accuracy. Land within ±1.5 for a BULLSEYE (+300). Consecutive good guesses build a streak multiplier.
  • 💡Hints. Reveal the US News rank, applicants, or enrollment — but each one lowers that round's max score.
  • ❤️Lives. You have 3 hearts. A miss worse than ±25 costs one. Lose all three and it's game over.
  • 📊The reveal. See where the school sits in the full distribution (histogram + boxplot) and its selectivity percentile among the pool.
  • 🏆Leaderboard. Beat the board, enter your 3 initials arcade-style, and compete on a separate board for each difficulty.
  • 🌗Light / dark. Flip themes anytime with the DARK MODE / LIGHT MODE switch.
Data: IPEDS 2024–25 admissions · US News ranked universe (~1,350 schools).
Acceptance rate = admitted ÷ applied.
